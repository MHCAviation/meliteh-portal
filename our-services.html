With our Background Check & Compliance services, we prioritize the safety and security of your organization by providing meticulous and comprehensive vetting processes. Our team understands the critical importance of accuracy and attention to detail in meeting the stringent requirements set forth by regulatory bodies. Operating in full compliance with Regulation: Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2015/1998 and amendment (EU) 2019/103, we ensure that our background screening processes align with the latest regulations. Our dedicated specialists possess the expertise and knowledge to navigate the complex landscape of background screening within the aviation industry.

We offer a range of background check services tailored to your specific needs, including basic or standard checks that can lead to EU enhanced checks. We conduct thorough five-year reference history checks, diligently addressing any gaps between employments to provide a complete picture of a candidate's background. Criminal record checks are an integral part of our screening process, enabling us to identify any potential risks or red flags. We adhere to international legislation and comply with the requirements of international aviation authorities within the European Union, ensuring that your screening procedures are aligned with the highest standards.

By partnering with us for Background Check & Compliance services, you benefit from our commitment to accuracy, precision, and adherence to regulatory requirements. We establish strong partnerships with our clients, working closely with your teams to provide trusted advice and maintain transparent communication throughout the screening process. Rest assured that all candidates undergo rigorous evaluations to ensure they meet the standards set by airport authorities, the Civil Aviation Authority (CAA), and the Department for Transport (DfT). Our meticulous approach to background screening leaves no room for error, allowing you to make informed decisions and maintain a secure environment for your organization.

Comprehensive Services
We offer a comprehensive range of contracting and payroll services to streamline your operations. Some of our key areas of expertise include:

  1. Employment Contracts: Our team assists in preparing accurate and compliant employment contracts tailored to your specific needs.
  2. Contractor and Service Company Agreements: We provide expert guidance and support in developing agreements that define the terms and conditions for contractors and service companies.
  3. Payroll Processing and Dedicated Payroll Consultant: With our payroll processing services, you can rely on our dedicated payroll consultant to handle all aspects of payroll management, ensuring accuracy and efficiency.
  4. Online Payslips and Distribution: We offer a user-friendly online platform for generating and distributing payslips, providing easy access to payroll information for employees.
  5. Personal and Company Expenses Administration: Our services extend to managing personal and company expenses administration, ensuring compliance with regulations and seamless processing.
  6. Compliance with Local and International Regulations: We ensure strict compliance with local and international labor laws, civil aviation requirements, and other relevant regulations.
  7. Tax and National Insurance Contribution: Our team is well-versed in tax and national insurance contribution regulations, ensuring accurate calculations and compliance.
  8. Not Limited by Union Agreements: We work independently of union agreements, providing flexibility and tailored solutions for your contracting and payroll needs.
  9. Regular Statements: We provide regular statements to keep you informed about payroll activities, facilitating transparency and financial oversight.
  10. Multi-Currency: Our services accommodate multi-currency requirements, enabling smooth payroll operations across different regions.

Efficient Online Support
Our user-friendly online tools are easily accessible and designed to support your daily payroll tasks efficiently. With our custom-made web-based and mobile-friendly invoice portal, connected with our payroll system, you can automate your payroll process and gain access to the client approval process in one centralized platform.

Leveraging Technology for Optimization
We utilize Microsoft Dynamics, an enterprise resource planning (ERP) application, to support our financial, payroll, and business decisions. This powerful tool provides data and statistics for resource and cost optimization, employee and contractor statistics, and bespoke monthly cost breakdown reports upon request.

Data Analysis and Reporting with Power BI
To offer valuable insights, we utilize Power BI for data analysis, customized key performance indicators (KPIs) based on your requirements, and monthly reports after each pay run. Power BI consolidates data sources, including Navision-Invoice portal-CRM system, to provide a comprehensive overview of your payroll and contracting data.
