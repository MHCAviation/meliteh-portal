At Meliteh, we are your trusted partner for comprehensive solutions in recruitment, background check & compliance, HR & payroll, and consulting services. Specializing in the Aviation, Maritime, and IT industries, we also extend our expertise to management, finances, and other sectors.  With the potential for expansion into other fields, we are dedicated to solving real-world problems for our clients by delivering customized and effective solutions that meet their unique needs.

At Meliteh, we understand that every client is different, and that's why we offer a personalized and flexible approach to recruitment and consulting. Our brand promise is to exceed your expectations by delivering results that are tailored to your requirements. Combining decades of experience with a fresh and innovative mindset, we differentiate ourselves from competitors and ensure your success.

Our brand personality embodies professionalism and approachability. We believe in building strong relationships with our clients based on trust and mutual respect. Our team is confident and knowledgeable, equipped to handle the complexities of the industries we serve, while remaining open and empathetic to your needs. As a premier provider of recruitment and consulting services, we position ourselves at the forefront of the industry. Our focus is on delivering quality and value to our clients, surpassing expectations with our unique and customized solutions. We pride ourselves on our personalized approach and unwavering commitment to excellence
