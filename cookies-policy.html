Cookies Policy

​This website uses cookies (digital footprints). Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (pc/ipad/smartphone etc.) to help the website provide a better user experience. Cookies enable your device to remember previous visits for a certain length of time which for example shortens loading time of the site. In general, cookies are used to retain user preferences, store anonymous information about how users use the website, and provide anonymized tracking data to third party applications like Google Analytics and facebook pixel to enable us track and monitor traffic on the website to help us understand user behavior better so that we can improve the website.
​
No personal information is stored about users and no information are shared with a third party. If you want to turn off cookies than we recommend that you do so in your browser settings. You can also visit the website aboutcookies.org that offers good information about cookies and instructions how to turn them off in all the main browsers.
